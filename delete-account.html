Delete Your Account

How to delete your Cliff Battle account and all associated data.

Last updated: 11 May 2026 App: com.cliffbattle.game

🎮 Delete Your Account Inside the App (Recommended)

Cliff Battle includes a built-in account deletion feature. This is the fastest and easiest way to remove your account and data.

Steps:

  1. Open the Cliff Battle app on your Android device
  2. Tap the Profile icon
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile screen
  4. Tap "Delete account and all data"
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and personal data will be permanently deleted immediately.

📧 Alternative: Delete via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to uassad716@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the phone number linked to your Cliff Battle account
  4. We will delete your account within 30 days and confirm via email

📋 What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your Cliff Battle account, the following personal data is permanently removed:

📊 What Data Is Retained

Some anonymized data may be retained to maintain fair leaderboards and prevent fraud:

  • Anonymized match statistics (no longer linked to you) — kept for leaderboard fairness
  • Aggregated gameplay data (e.g. average match duration) — used for app improvements
  • Legal records if required by law (e.g. payment transactions for tax compliance) — retained per German tax law (max. 10 years for financial records, per § 147 AO)
Note: Anonymized data cannot be linked back to you personally and is therefore not considered personal data under GDPR.

🔒 What Happens After Deletion

  • You will no longer be able to log in with your phone number
  • Your username becomes available for new users
  • Your Crown balance is forfeited and cannot be recovered
  • You can create a new account at any time with the same or different phone number
  • We send a confirmation email (only for email-based deletion requests)
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to use any remaining Crowns or take screenshots of your stats before deleting.
